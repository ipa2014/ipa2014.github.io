Accomodation

Hotels
In and arround Wageningen there are several hotels. As hotel capacity is limited, we have made block reservations for attendees of IPA 2014 with all hotels in Wageningen and Ede. Originally the block reservation would expire on May 21, 2014 but we have been able to extend this deadline. We would like to advise you to book your Wageningen or Ede hotel before the June 3, 2014 (new deadline), in order to be ellegible for the IPA 2014 reservation. After this date, we cannot guarantee any hotel rooms in the close vicinity of Wageningen anymore and you may have to travel to Utrecht or Arnhem.

Because of the block reservation, it is not possible to book your hotelroom through the hotel websites. These will tell you that the hotel is fully booked. Please contact the hotels directly through email or by telephone to make your reservation. For more information on rates, contact details of the various hotels and travel time to the conference venue please scroll down.

Note : Block reservations for the preconference course have been made with hotel 'De Reehorst'.

Any questions related to hotel reservations? Please contact us at ipa2014@wur.nl.
